⚡ Failover Cascade

1
VM1 fails → VM2 claims VIP (stays in DC1)  ~1–2s
2
VM1 + VM2 fail → VM3 claims VIP (moves to DC2)  ~2–3s
3
DC1 entirely dark → VM3 = MASTER, VM4 = BACKUP  ~3s
4
Dark fibre drops → Split-brain risk. Mitigated by nopreempt + priority gap + track scripts.
